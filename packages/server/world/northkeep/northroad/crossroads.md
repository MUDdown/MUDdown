# The Crossroads

Four paths meet at a muddy junction. A [stone marker](cmd:examine marker)
stands at the center, its carved directions half-worn by weather. To the
east, a low farmstead is visible. Dense trees crowd the western path.
Northward, the road narrows toward a dark hillside.
