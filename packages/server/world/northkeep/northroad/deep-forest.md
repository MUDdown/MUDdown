# Deep Forest

The trees here are ancient and gnarled, their roots breaking through the
earth in twisted arcs. Almost no light reaches the forest floor. The air
is thick with the scent of moss and decay. A strange [glowing mushroom](cmd:examine mushroom)
dots the base of the largest trunk, casting a faint blue light.

## Exits
- [South](go:south) — Forest Edge

## Present
- A [grey wolf](npc:wolf) watches you from the shadows, ears flat.

## Items
- A [glowing mushroom](item:mushroom) pulses with a soft blue light.
- A [tattered cloak](item:cloak) is caught on a low branch.
